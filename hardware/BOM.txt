List of material needed:

Pre-assembled:
- Commercial Slide-Stroke Z-Axis CNC linear actuator with at least 4 kg/cm torque (e.g. https://www.banggood.com/150mm-Slide-Stroke-Z-Axis-Mini-CNC-Linear-Motion-Milling-Engraving-Machine-p-1124302.html)
- Motor control driver for NEMA 17 motors (e.g. https://www.banggood.com/HANPOSE-TB6600-Stepper-Motor-Driver-4A-DC9-42V-For-Nema-17-or-23-or-34-Stepper-Motor-CNC-Engraving-Machine-p-1415418.html)
- Arduino Mega 2560 (for other models just change pin assignment)
- Display LCD 1602 I2C (e.g https://www.dfrobot.com/product-135.html)
- 9V 1A power supply

Electronics:
- CZL635 load cell (0-5Kg)
- INA125P amplifier
- 1 x 47ohm resistance
- 3 x buttons
- 1 x switch
- 1 x 2.5V LED

- metal profile 12x8x140 to maintain the silde stroke vertically aligned
